Glass Art

by: Raymond Martinez

These are not on glass but I wanted to throw them in here anyways. Once I paint more I will dedicate a site for oils and water colors!

All art was done by me.
You can check them out on my instagram page

X

I started drawing on the bathroom mirror to remind my nephew to wash his hands and then it went on from there. The superheroes are on bathroom mirrors and I used dry erase markers. I drew the horror movie characters on the front windows for Halloween and I used chalk pens.

Calvin and Hobbes, The Nightmare Before Christmas and The Looney Tunes Thanksgiving were painted with acrylics.
